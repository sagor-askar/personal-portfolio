Project Overview

This website was developed for NGIT (Next Generation IT), an independent, non-profit, non-political organization. The platform reflects NGIT's dedication to advancing science and engineering research at the undergraduate level — from nurturing innovative concepts and publishing scholarly articles to facilitating skill development and higher education support As the technical solution architect for the site, I crafted a clean, organized, and responsive interface that highlights NGIT’s mission, research initiatives, leadership team, and contact pathways. The site is designed to empower collaboration between budding researchers and national/international experts, aligning with NGIT’s vision of fostering impactful, ethically responsible, and globally relevant scientific inquiry
